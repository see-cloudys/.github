### 👋 About Us

We’re a chill community that’s obsessed with cloud technology.  
We build open-source tools, learn together, and make complex things feel simple.

We believe: **The best tech isn’t just powerful — it’s clear and fun to use.**

### 🌟 What We Do

- 🛠️ Create open-source cloud tools & libraries  
- 📚 Share knowledge through blogs, workshops, and meetups  
- 🤝 Collaborate with developers from Indonesia and around the world  
- 🎯 Focus on **simplicity**, **clarity**, and **real impact**
